Gallo Pinto Recipe

Classic Costa Rican Gallo Pinto!

Classic Costa Rican nutritous breakfast sreved with flavorfull spices and sour cream to gain the energy for your day.

Ingridients

  • Beans
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Sweet Chile

Steps

  1. Add onionm garlic and sweet chile to pan and fry.
  2. Add beans and rice to pan
  3. Mix
  4. Cook
  5. Serve
